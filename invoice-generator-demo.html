How to Use

1. Create New Invoice: Click "New Invoice" to start with a blank invoice template.

2. Fill Details: Enter company information, client details, and add invoice items.

3. Review & Save: Preview your invoice and save it to history for future reference.

4. Complete Invoice: Use the "Complete" feature to finalize the invoice, lock editing, and generate PDF.

5. Manage History: Load previous invoices, make copies, or delete old records.
