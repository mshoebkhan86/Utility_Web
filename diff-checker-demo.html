🚀 Quick Start Guide

  1. Enter Text: Paste or type your original text in the left panel and modified text in the right panel
  2. Choose Algorithm: Select Myers (default), Patience, or Histogram diff algorithm
  3. Set Options: Configure ignore case, whitespace, and other comparison settings
  4. Compare: Click "Compare" to see the differences highlighted in your chosen view mode
  5. Analyze Results: Review additions (green), deletions (red), and statistics
  6. Export: Save your results or load/save text files as needed
