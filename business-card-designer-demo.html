🚀 Getting Started

  • Choose a Template: Select from professional, modern, creative, or minimal designs
  • Add Text: Click "Add Text" to create text elements, then customize font, size, and color
  • Add Images: Upload your logo or photos using the "Add Image" button
  • Customize Design: Change background colors or add background images

🎨 Design Tools

  • Text Editing: Select text elements to modify content, font, size, color, and style
  • Alignment: Use left, center, right alignment buttons for precise positioning
  • Layering: Bring elements forward or send them backward to control overlap
  • Drag & Drop: Click and drag elements to reposition them on the card

💾 Saving & Exporting

  • Save: Download your design as a JSON file to continue editing later
  • Load: Upload a previously saved JSON file to resume editing
  • Export: Generate a high-resolution PNG image ready for printing

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Z: Undo last action
  • Ctrl+Y: Redo last undone action
  • Delete: Remove selected element

ℹ️ About Business Card Designer

×

The Business Card Designer is a comprehensive tool for creating professional business cards with ease. Whether you're a freelancer, entrepreneur, or business professional, this tool provides everything you need to design stunning cards.

🌟 Key Features

  • 🎨 Professional Templates: Choose from carefully designed templates
  • 📝 Rich Text Editor: Full control over fonts, sizes, colors, and styles
  • 🖼️ Image Support: Add logos, photos, and graphics
  • 🎯 Precise Positioning: Drag and drop with alignment tools
  • 📐 Standard Size: 3.5" × 2" business card format
  • 📤 High-Quality Export: 300 DPI print-ready images
  • 💾 Save & Load: Continue your work anytime
  • ↶ Undo/Redo: Full history management
